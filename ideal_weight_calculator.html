What is Ideal Weight?

Ideal body weight (IBW) is a measure of what a person should weigh based on their height and gender. Different formulas calculate IBW differently, taking into account various factors and medical perspectives.

Why Multiple Formulas?

Different formulas were developed by various researchers over the years. Each has its own methodology and assumptions, which is why our calculator shows results from multiple formulas for comparison.

BMI Method

The BMI (Body Mass Index) method calculates a healthy weight range based on a BMI of 18.5 to 25, which is considered the healthy range for most adults.

Important Note

These calculations are estimates. Individual factors such as muscle mass, bone density, and overall fitness level also play important roles in determining an appropriate weight.
