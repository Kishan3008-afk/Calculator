Frequently Asked Questions

How is daily caloric need calculated? ‚ñº
We use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, which is one of the most accurate formulas for calculating Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR). This is then multiplied by an activity factor based on your exercise level to determine your total daily energy expenditure.
Why is saturated fat limited? ‚ñº
High saturated fat intake is associated with increased LDL cholesterol levels, which increases the risk of heart disease. Health organizations recommend limiting saturated fat to less than 10% of daily calories as a preventive measure.
Can I adjust my fat intake recommendations? ‚ñº
While our calculator provides evidence-based recommendations, individual needs may vary. Consult with a registered dietitian or nutritionist for personalized advice based on your health conditions and goals.
What is the difference between US and Metric units? ‚ñº
US units use inches for height and pounds for weight, while Metric units use centimeters and kilograms. The calculator automatically converts between units and provides the same accurate results regardless of which system you choose.
Is this calculator suitable for everyone? ‚ñº
This calculator is designed for adults aged 18-80 in generally good health. If you have medical conditions, are pregnant, or follow a specific diet, please consult with a healthcare professional for personalized recommendations.
